Defeat the Evil Wizard
A turn-based battle game built in Python. You choose a hero and fight the Evil Wizard.

Characters
You can choose from 4 character classes - Warrior, Mage, Archer and Paladin. Each one has different stats and 2 unique special abilities.

How to play
Each turn you can attack, use a special ability, heal, or view your stats. The wizard regenerates health every turn so you need to keep the pressure on. The game ends when either you or the wizard runs out of health.
